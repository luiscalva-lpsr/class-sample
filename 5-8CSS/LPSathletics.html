Leadership Public Schools provide students the opportunity to play in some of the best teams for teens in the bay area. Our teams are well trained, diciplined and filled with the best young talent around. Signing up to one of our teams will be one of the best experiences of your highschool life, not to mention you will meet new people and develope your skills in whatever sport you are interested in.

To be able to sign up for a sport talk to whichever teacher is in charge of the sport. Further information will be given to students before practice begins.

Our baseball team has had their best season ever. The team recently played for the championship game and became runner ups.

Students that show great talent in a sport could be given scholarships for a big school, joining a sport can help be scouted.
